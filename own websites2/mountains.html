Manali

A gift of the himalayas to the world, Manali is a beautifull township. It's famous for there cool climate and snow-capped mountains.


Jammu & Kashmir

Jammu & Kashmir is known as the paradise on Earth because it's has amazing landscape and scienic beauty.It has beautifull snow covered mountains,green valley and natural lakes.


Kedarnath

Keadrnath is the most remote of the four Chota Char Dham pilgrimage sites. It is located in the Himalayas about (11,755ft) anove sea level near the Chorabari Glacier.


Kufri

Kufri is a small hill station located in the shimla district. The scenic view and snow-capped landscapes atrract tourists from all part of the country and abroad.


Drajeeling

Darjeeling is famous throughout the world for the tea it grows and the great view of the Kanchenjunga range of the mountains that it offers. It is also know for it's richness in culture and in nature heritage and the famous toy train that has been declared a UN heritage.


Leh-ladakh

Ladakh a word which means "land of high passes". Ladakh is most famous for there breattaking landscapes, the crystal clear skies, the highest mountain passes, thrilling adventures activites, Buddhist monastries and festivals.


Mount-abu

Serving as a source of comfort from the sultry desert climate of Rajasthan, Mount-abu is the state's only hill station stands at a height of 1722 metres above the sea level, and is embraced by lush green hills of the Aravalli range.
